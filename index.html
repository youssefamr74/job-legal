How Jōb uses your Gmail data

Jōb connects to Gmail to automatically detect and import your job application emails. Here's exactly what we do and don't do with your data:

  • We read emails to identify job application confirmations, interview invites, and rejections
  • We extract only the subject line, sender, and date — never the full email body
  • We never store, sell, or share your email content with third parties
  • Gmail connection is optional — you can add applications manually instead
  • You can disconnect Gmail and delete all your data at any time from the app
