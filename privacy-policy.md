# Privacy Policy for Jōb

**Last updated: June 2026**

Jōb ("we", "our", "the app") is a job search tracking application. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

## 1. Information We Collect

### Account Information
When you register, we collect your email address, name, and country. If you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email from your Google account.

### Gmail Data
With your explicit consent, Jōb connects to your Gmail account to read job-related emails. We use this access exclusively to identify and classify application status updates (such as application confirmations, assessment invitations, interview scheduling, offers, and rejections). We do not read, store, or process personal, financial, or unrelated emails.

Specifically, we access:
- Email subject lines and body content of messages identified as job-related
- Sender information to identify companies and recruiters
- Timestamps to build your application timeline

We do not access emails outside this scope, and we never read or store emails from your Sent folder for classification purposes.

### Application Data
We store the application records derived from your Gmail data, including company names, role titles, application status, dates, and any manual edits you make.

### Usage Data
We collect basic usage analytics (such as which screens you visit and how often you use the app) to improve the product.

### Payment Information
Jōb uses RevenueCat and the Apple App Store / Google Play Store to process subscription payments. We do not collect, store, or have access to your credit card or payment details. Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple, Google, and RevenueCat in accordance with their own privacy policies.

### CV/Resume Data (Pro feature, optional)
If you choose to upload your CV or resume, we store the file securely and generate a condensed summary of your background (skills, experience, education) to power more personalized responses from the AI companion feature. This is entirely optional — the app's core functionality works without it. You can remove your uploaded CV at any time from your profile, which permanently deletes the file and its summary from our systems.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and maintain the core functionality of automatically tracking your job applications
- Generate insights and analytics about your job search (such as response rates and best times to apply)
- Provide AI-powered companion features that reference your application data
- Send you optional daily reminder notifications, if enabled
- Improve and develop the app

## 3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your Gmail content or application data with third parties, except:
- With Google, to authenticate and retrieve your Gmail data (per Google's API Services User Data Policy)
- With our cloud infrastructure providers (such as our hosting and database providers) strictly to operate the app
- With RevenueCat, to manage subscription status (RevenueCat does not receive your Gmail data)
- With Anthropic, to power the AI companion feature, where relevant application data may be sent to generate responses to your questions

## 4. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your associated application data, Gmail tokens, and personal information within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

## 5. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access the data we hold about you
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your account and associated data
- Revoke Gmail access at any time via your Google Account security settings or within the app

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

## 6. Google API Services User Data Policy

Jōb's use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the [Google API Services User Data Policy](https://developers.google.com/terms/api-services-user-data-policy), including the Limited Use requirements. We do not use Gmail data for advertising purposes, and we do not allow humans to read your email data except in the limited circumstances permitted by Google's policy (such as with your consent, for security purposes, or to comply with the law).

## 7. Data Security

We use industry-standard measures (encryption in transit, secure authentication tokens, and access controls) to protect your data. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 8. Children's Privacy

Jōb is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of material changes through the app or via email.
